Let's see what goals this entrepreneur has achieved:

  • Elon musk started programming using Commodore_VIC-20 when he was only 10
  • He sold his first video game, Blast, to PC and Office Technology magazine for $500 After 2 years of programming experience.
  • He moved to Canada from South Africa since Elon Musk decide to attend a college in U.S.A.
  • In 1995, Elon Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded web software company Zip2. They housed the venture at a small rented office in Palo Alto. They could only afford one computer and, according to Musk, "The website was up during the day and I was coding it at night, seven days a week, all the time.
  • Elon Musk graduated from University of Pennsylvania with BS and BA degrees when he was 26.
  • Compaq acquired Zip2 for $307 million in cash in February 1999. Musk received $22 million for his 7 percent share from the sale.s
  • The same year he sold his company, Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company, with $10 million from the sale of Zip2. Later X.com merged with Paypal, which focus on money-transfer service. In October 2002, PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock, of which Musk received $165 million.
  • Tesla, Inc. was incorporated in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning, who financed the company until the Series A round of funding. Both men played active roles in the company's early development prior to Musk's involvement.Musk led the Series A round of investment in February 2004, joining Tesla's board of directors as its chairman.
  • Musk assumed leadership of the company as CEO and product architect in 2008, positions he still holds today. As of 2019, Elon Musk is the longest tenured CEO of any automotive manufacturer globally.
  • In an interview, Musk favored building a more affordable Tesla model, at around $30,000. Initial deliveries began in 2017, with the $35,000 base model becoming available in February 2019. As of March 2020, the Tesla Model 3 is the world's best-selling electric car, with more than 500,000 units delivered. Musk originally intended to name the Model 3 the Model E but was blocked by Ford which held the trademark, with Musk concluding that "Ford was killing SEX".

"I always invest my own money in the companies that I create. I don't believe in the whole thing of just using other people's money. I don't think that's right. I'm not going to ask other people to invest in something if I'm not prepared to do so myself."
